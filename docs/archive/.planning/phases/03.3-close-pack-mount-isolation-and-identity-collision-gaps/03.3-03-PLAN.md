<objective>
Wire identity forwarding through the bridge service, update the OSS /runs route to pass external user identity, and implement full local_compose provider parity with Daytona's mount isolation pattern.

Purpose: The bridge currently hardcodes `sender_id: "minerva"` (picoclaw_bridge_service.py line 319), breaking per-user conversation scoping in Picoclaw. The local_compose provider still writes config.json inside `/workspace/pack` and lacks mount isolation. This plan fixes both, completing the identity and isolation story across all providers.

Output: Bridge with sender_id/session_id forwarding, OSS route with identity passthrough, local_compose with full isolation parity.
</objective>
