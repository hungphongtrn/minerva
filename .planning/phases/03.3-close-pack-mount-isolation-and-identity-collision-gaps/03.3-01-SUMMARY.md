## Decisions Made

- **End-user identity separation**: Completely separate `external_identities` table from `users` table to prevent privilege escalation
- **Workspace-scoped resolution**: All end-users resolve to the developer's workspace via `MINERVA_WORKSPACE_ID`, not their own isolated workspace
- **Composite key design**: `(workspace_id, external_user_id)` ensures two deployments can both have `user-123` without collision
- **Guest handling**: Guests (matching `GUEST_ID`) get no DB record, ensuring no session continuity or identity pollution
- **Startup validation**: Preflight check enforces `MINERVA_WORKSPACE_ID` is set and points to a workspace with registered packs
